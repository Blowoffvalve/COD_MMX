The 2023 Peoples Democratic Party, PDP, presidential candidate, Atiku Abubakar, has celebrated Pastor Enoch Adeboye, the General Overseer of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, RCCG, on his 82nd birthday.

Atiku described Adeboye as a respected religious reader who has guided many in their faith.

Posting on X, Atiku wished Pastor Adeboye many more years of success.

According to Adeboye: “Pastor Enoch Adeboye is a respected religious leader who has guided many in their faith.

“As he celebrates his birthday today, I, along with many others, wish him more years of success in his divine assignment. -AA”

Papa G.O. as he’s popularly referred to by his followers clocked 82 years on Saturday.
